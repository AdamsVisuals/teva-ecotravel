Tarangire's Unique Ecosystem

Often overshadowed by the Serengeti, Tarangire National Park offers one of Africa's most concentrated wildlife viewing experiences, particularly during the dry season when animals congregate around the Tarangire River.

Our eco-tours are designed to minimize impact while maximizing your connection with this extraordinary landscape and its majestic elephant herds.
