Ngorongoro Wildlife

Witness an astonishing diversity of species

Black Rhinos

Critically endangered and closely monitored within the crater.

Best seen: Year-round

Lions

One of the densest lion populations in Africa resides here.

Best seen: June - October

Hippos

Watch pods of hippos lounging in crater waterholes.

Best seen: Wet season

Flamingos

Lake Magadi attracts thousands of pink flamingos in season.

Peak: November - April
