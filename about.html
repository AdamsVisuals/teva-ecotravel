Our Story

Teva EcoTravel was born from a shared passion for conservation and authentic cultural experiences. Founded by wildlife biologists Sarah and Michael in 2010, we started with just three eco-conscious tours in Tanzania.

Today, we operate across East Africa while maintaining our commitment to low-impact travel that benefits local communities and protects fragile ecosystems.

12+ Years in Eco-Tourism
50+ Local Team Members
100% Carbon Neutral

Our Core Values

Guiding principles that shape every Teva EcoTravel experience

Environmental Stewardship

We minimize our ecological footprint through sustainable practices, from solar-powered camps to plastic-free operations.

Community Partnership

85% of our staff are local hires, and we invest 15% of profits back into community development projects.

Authentic Education

Our expert guides provide deep insights into ecosystems and cultures, creating meaningful connections.

Conservation First

Every tour directly funds wildlife protection and habitat restoration initiatives.
