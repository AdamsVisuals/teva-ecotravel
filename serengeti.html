Wildlife You May Encounter

The Serengeti is home to Africa's most iconic species

Big Cats

Lions, leopards, and cheetahs thrive in the Serengeti's vast plains.

Best seen: Year-round

Great Migration

Over 1.5 million wildebeest and zebras complete this annual cycle.

Peak: June - October

Elephants

Herds of African elephants roam the northern woodlands.

Best seen: Dry season

Birdlife

Over 500 bird species including ostriches and vultures.

Peak: November - April
