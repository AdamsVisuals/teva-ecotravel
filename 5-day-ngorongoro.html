Detailed Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival - Lake Manyara National Park

Arrive at Kilimanjaro International Airport where you'll be met by your guide. Transfer to Lake Manyara National Park, known for its tree-climbing lions and large elephant herds. Afternoon game drive followed by dinner at your lodge overlooking the Rift Valley.

Accommodation: Lake Manyara Serena Lodge
Meals: Lunch, Dinner
Day 2

Lake Manyara to Ngorongoro Conservation Area

Morning game drive in Lake Manyara before traveling to the Ngorongoro Conservation Area. Stop at a Maasai village to learn about this iconic pastoralist culture. Arrive at your lodge on the crater rim in time for sunset with spectacular views into the crater.

Accommodation: Ngorongoro Serena Safari Lodge
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Day 3

Ngorongoro Crater Full Day

Descend 600m into the crater floor for a full day of wildlife viewing. The crater's high walls create a natural enclosure for about 25,000 animals, including all the Big Five. Picnic lunch by a hippo pool. Return to your lodge in the late afternoon.

Accommodation: Ngorongoro Serena Safari Lodge
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Day 4

Ngorongoro Crater - Second Game Drive

Morning descent into the crater for another spectacular game drive, focusing on areas you may have missed the previous day. Afternoon at leisure at the lodge with optional activities like nature walks or cultural visits.

Accommodation: Ngorongoro Serena Safari Lodge
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Day 5

Departure

After breakfast, transfer back to Arusha or Kilimanjaro Airport for your departure flight, with optional stop at a local market for souvenir shopping.

Accommodation: None
Meals: Breakfast
